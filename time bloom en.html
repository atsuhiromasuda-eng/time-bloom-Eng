Time Bloom

— Grow or Let Go? —
🌱 🌿 🌸 🥀
🎮 How to Play Start with 60 seconds on the clock (max 120s).
Swipe to swap blocks. Match 3 or more of the same color to clear them.
Clearing blocks earns score and recovers time.
Combos (chain reactions) multiply your time recovery!
If no valid moves remain, it's game over.

🌱 Growth System Blocks grow automatically over time:
🌱Sprout (10pt +1s) → 🌿Leaf (30pt +2s) →
🌸Flower (80pt +3s) → 🥀Wilted (obstacle -5s)
Higher growth = bigger rewards and time recovery,
but wilted flowers become immovable obstacles with a -5s penalty.
If 3 wilted flowers line up (horizontal/vertical), they auto-clear
but cost -200pts & -15s penalty!
※Even with 4+ in a row, only the first 3 are cleared.

⚠️ Speed Up (Rising Difficulty) Growth speed increases as the game progresses:
・Every 10 cumulative wilted flowers: +0.1× speed
・Every 90 seconds of play: +0.1× speed
・Speed never decreases once raised
Example: 20 wilted + 3min elapsed → ×1.4 speed

⏱ Items Earn an item when the timer exceeds 120 seconds!
Time is paused while using items.

🧹Vacuum — Remove all wilted flowers instantly.
❄️Ice — Tap to freeze a 4×4 area for 60 seconds (stops growth).
💣Bomb — Tap to destroy a 2×2 area. No time bonus. Can't destroy wilted.
🌀Vortex — Rewind all growth by 10s. Flower→Leaf→Sprout. Wilted stays wilted.
⚡Lightning — Destroy 1-5 random blocks. Can hit wilted flowers too. It's a gamble!

🌈 Rainbow Bonus Block A rare special block that appears occasionally.
Acts as a wildcard — matches any color.
When cleared, wipes out 2 random rows for a massive clear!
